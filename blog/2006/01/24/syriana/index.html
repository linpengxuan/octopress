SYRIANA

Jan 24th, 2006

Sitting put, with a cold and beard face, he made some noise out of his mouth. If anything happens to me or my family, an accident, an accusation, anything, then first your son will disappear, his body will never be found. Then your wife. Her body will never be found either. This is guaranteed. Then, whatever is the most dangerous thing you do in your life, it might be flying in a small plane, it might be walking to the bank, you will be killed. Do you understand what I’m saying? I want you to acknowledge that you do understand so that we’re clear and there won’t be any mistakes. In a cruel, cold yet confidence voice, those lines mirrors an attitude from a CIA agent who has been working for his country for decades, yet found out been betrayed by the same employer. Wrath and anger would not be enough to describe what he could feel, however, still what he would do later prove him as a patriot and man of love, peace and equality.

Yes, those are lines from Syriana, one of triology political movies( I rated accordingly, includes “the Manchuria’s Candidate””The Fahrenheit 911”), has push this movie into a words-killing episode. We might be sick of typical Hollywood things, full of explosion, heroism, one-man-save-the-world things or Tom Cruise. Yet for this kind, I am sure, by unveiling the mysterious agency, we will be exposed to a person more than real, a person of vengeance set in an era, the oil price skyrocketing high. What else can one man ask for more? Define real and cool before u say there might be one more than those. Harry Potter, Narnia are fairy tales, this is novel damn suitable for adults.

Remember this movie, one deserve your second time tasting to enjoy more.
